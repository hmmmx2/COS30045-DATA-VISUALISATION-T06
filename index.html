About This Dashboard

This dashboard visualizes TV energy consumption data and Australian electricity spot prices. It includes four interactive D3.js charts: a scatter plot showing the relationship between star ratings and energy consumption, a line chart tracking electricity prices from 1998-2024, a bar chart comparing energy use by screen technology for 55-inch TVs, and a donut chart showing overall energy consumption by screen type.
